## 1. Change Management Plan
Address the human side of the deployment.

### 1.1 Impact Assessment
- Who will be most affected by this change?
- What current processes are being replaced or retired?
- Resistance mitigation strategy: [Prompt]

### 1.2 Communication Timeline
- **T-4 Weeks:** Initial announcement (Coming Soon).
- **T-2 Weeks:** Detailed changes & schedule generic training.
- **T-0 (Launch):** "We are live" announcement + Support channels.
- **T+2 Weeks:** Follow-up survey.

## 3. Adoption Metrics
How do we measure if people are actually using the system?

- **Metric 1:** Daily Active Users (DAU)
- **Metric 2:** Completion rate of key workflows
- **Metric 3:** Support ticket volume related to "How do I..."
